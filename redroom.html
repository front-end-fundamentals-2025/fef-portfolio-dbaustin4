Project Details


Problem Statement


What challenge or need this project addresses.

The game is intended primarily for listeners 35 years of age and older on Qmusic, which is an older audience. This group likes to spend their free time participating in involved and thought-provoking activities, and they are probably aware of the radio station's services. Their interests lie in nostalgic yet logical entertainment, which makes them perfect targets for a point-and-click escape game.
