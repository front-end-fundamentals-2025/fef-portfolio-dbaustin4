The Red Room


  • 2D Point and Click Puzzle Escape Game
  • In Process of Creation for DPG Media
  • Will be Launched as an HTML-5
  • In House Game Development for Denda Games

Project Details


Problem Statement


What challenge or need this project addresses.

The game is intended primarily for listeners 35 years of age and older on Qmusic, which is an older audience. This group likes to spend their free time participating in involved and thought-provoking activities, and they are probably aware of the radio station's services. Their interests lie in nostalgic yet logical entertainment, which makes them perfect targets for a point-and-click escape game.


Solution & Goals


  • Researched escape games to study mechanics, pacing, and engagement.
  • Explored art styles and their impact on user experience.
  • Researched puzzle mechanics tailored to the target audience

Key Features

  • 28 Days of Unique Gameplay
  • 2 Unique Forms of Gameplay
  • Follow Along an Engaging Storyline
  • Brain-Training Methodology
